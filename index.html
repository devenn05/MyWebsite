Based out of Seattle Washington, Gavin Maher, professionally known as gavn!, is a singer and rapper from the underground rap scene known for his incredible hooks and vocal range on tracks like “Ecstasy!”, “Come Thru” and “Havoc!”. He arrived on SoundCloud by making songs with Kody Lavigne, SypSki and Yung 808. Gavn! continues making tracks that are as catchy as they are deep and thoughtful.
